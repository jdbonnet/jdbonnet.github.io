Breakfast

Tomatoes and Watermelon

Chop up tomatoes and watermelon into 1¼ cube, add a pinch of salt, 1 tbs of olive oil and 2 tbs of sherry vinegar. Mix together, add a cup of feta cheese crumbles on top.Optional:Add eggs, any style.

Lunch

Cucumber Soup

Peel, seed and chop 5 cucumbers and 4 scallions and mix in a food processor. Add 1 cup of cilantro, 8 oz of sour cream, ¾ cup of buttermilk, ¼ tsp cayenne pepper, 2 tsp of salt and puree until smooth. Serve the soup chilled with your choice of salsa.

Dinner

Sauteed zucchini

Heat one tbs of olive oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add 2 cloves of garlic finely chopped and one tbs of oregano, thinly slice 3 zucchinis and add to the skillet. Cook, toss gently, 10-12 mins adding a pinch of salt and pepper.

Dessert

Raspberry S’mores

Regular S’more with a twist- Place your graham crackers, chocolate and marhmallow on foil and broil for 30-60 seconds- until marshmallows are golden brown. Carefully remove the foil from the oven and place a raspberry on top of the marshmallow, place the graham cracker on top and enjoy!
